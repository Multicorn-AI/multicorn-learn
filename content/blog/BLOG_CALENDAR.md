## Content Guidelines

### Each Post Should Include:

- **SEO keywords** identified before writing
- **Follows existing blog template** and styling (see `introducing-multicorn-shield.mdx`)
- **CTA at the end** to try Shield (link to `/shield` or `https://app.multicorn.ai/signup`)
- **Reference to proxy quickstart** (`/docs/mcp-proxy`) where relevant

### Persona Relevance

- **Sarah**: Blog posts are how she understands the product. Without them, the landing page alone may not be enough.
- **Priya**: Tutorial posts are what get open-source contributors to actually try the product.
- **The Team (acquirer)**: A content strategy with SEO keywords and a publishing cadence shows mature go-to-market thinking.

## Status Tracking

- **Published**: Post is live on `/blog`
- **In Progress**: Currently being written
- **Planned**: Queued for future writing
