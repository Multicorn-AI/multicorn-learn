This lesson will introduce **analytics** and **crash reporting** as product feedback loops, **phased release** (rollout percentage), and what it means to ship a follow-up build when OS requirements change. It will not prescribe a single vendor; the goal is to understand categories of tools and dashboard labels.

**Checkpoint (stub):** You will be able to name one metric to watch in week one and one reason to ship a new version that is not “new features.”
