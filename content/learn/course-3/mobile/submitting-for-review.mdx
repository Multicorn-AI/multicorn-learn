This lesson will cover **App Review** and **Play policy** at a high level: privacy, permissions, in-app purchase rules, and broken or placeholder content. It will set realistic **timelines** and what to do when a build is **rejected** (read the message, fix, resubmit), without promising same-day approval.

**Checkpoint (stub):** You will have a one-page mental checklist for “ready to send to review.”
