This lesson will walk the **Apple Developer Program**, **provisioning** (so Apple knows which devices and which app ID you are using), and **code signing** in concept-first terms, then a minimal **archive** path you can follow in Xcode regardless of whether you used React Native, Flutter, or Expo to generate a native project.

**Checkpoint (stub):** You will confirm you can open the right project in Xcode and name the two places Apple expects metadata before upload.
